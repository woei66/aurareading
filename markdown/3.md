**Question:** Which trees are particularly helpful to the human aura?

**Answer:** Many trees possess a balanced, life-giving aura that can cleanse, restore, and specifically benefit the human energy field. To gain full benefit, the tree should ideally be taller than a single-storey house, and one should sit under its canopy for an hour or more with a relaxed, welcoming attitude.

Here are trees noted for their specific auric qualities:
*   **Robinia:** Refreshes all levels of your being.
*   **Willow:** Alleviates head tension and confusion.
*   **Birch:** Restores and balances human traits.
*   **Cedar:** Enhances your connection to Mother Nature.
*   **Chestnut:** Aids in release from a self-imposed poor image.
*   **Fruit Trees:** Open you to a sense of new growth and life.
*   **Magnolia:** Stimulates the purity of Self.
*   **Maple:** Aids in the preparation for major change.
*   **Oak:** Enhances wisdom for self-analysis and self-counselling.
*   **Pine:** Cleanses and balances the aura by absorbing negativity.
*   **Poplar:** Replenishes the vital auric energy flow.

Walking through a forest, especially one alive with birds, allows your aura to embrace the doe-skin brown, copper, dark brown, lemon yellow, and mulberry pink energies of nature. Even if you live in an apartment, placing several potted trees on a balcony allows a breeze to carry their auric energy indoors for a refreshing experience. Remember, a tree's own aura—often seen as yellow gold and soft green outlining its canopy—remains in a state of perfect balance, offering a stabilizing force for our own energy fields.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Aura reading work?

The app analyzes your uploaded full-body photo and generates a symbolic Aura image and interpretation for entertainment purposes only. It does not provide medical or scientific diagnosis.

Is my photo stored on the server?

No. Your photo is uploaded temporarily for processing and deleted immediately after the Aura image is generated. We do not store or share your photos.

How can I restore my Aura history if I change my device?

Aura images and reports are stored locally on your device. Please back up important images yourself before switching devices, as they cannot be recovered from our server.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your App Store or Google Play account settings. You can cancel anytime from your device's subscription management page.
